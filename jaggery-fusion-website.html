Our Story

From sugarcane fields to your table — with nothing added, nothing hidden.

Jaggery Fusion is produced by CAE Process Technologies, using sugarcane grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. Our process stays close to tradition: no refined sugar, no artificial colours, no chemical clarifying agents.

What comes out is jaggery the way it was meant to be — rich in iron, calcium, magnesium, potassium and phosphorus, with the taste and aroma that mass-processed sugar simply can't replicate.

We built our manufacturing process to meet both Indian food safety standards and the compliance expectations of international buyers, so the same quality that reaches a household in India can reach a distributor's shelf in Europe.
